Welcome to EDM-lab

Environmental Durability Metals Laboratory(EDM Lab) focuses on corrosion resistance, hydrogen embrittlement, and high-strength metallic materials, particularly for high performance applications. Our research aims to enhance material durability and reliability under specific environmental conditions.

Our Research & Motivation

Main Research Area

The EDM Lab aims to develop next-generation metallic materials with enhanced durability for automotive, energy, and industrial applications, ensuring sustainability and performance reliability.

- Hydrogen Embrittlement-Resistant Alloys & Process Optimization

- Corrosion Resistance & Anti - Rust Property Evaluation

- Hydrogen Quantification & Diffusion Analysis

- Durability Assessment in Complex Environments
